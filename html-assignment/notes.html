Five Tags & Their Descriptions

Main tag:
This is the primary content of the page.
Nav tag:
This is the navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website.
h1 tag:
This is the most important heading of the page.
P tag:
This tag is used for adding a paragraph.
Cite tag:
A citation for another source, often used with quotations.
